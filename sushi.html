Menu

Nigiri Sushi

This is one of the most recognizable types of sushi. It consists of a small hand-pressed mound of vinegared rice with a slice of raw or cooked seafood (like tuna, salmon, or shrimp) on top. It's often garnished with wasabi and served with soy sauce.

Price: Rs.1299

Maki Sushi

Maki sushi is made by rolling rice, fish, and vegetables in a sheet of seaweed (nori). It's cut into bite-sized pieces and often served with pickled ginger and wasabi. Some popular maki rolls include California rolls, spicy tuna rolls, and dragon rolls.

Price: Rs.1349

Temaki Sushi

Also known as hand rolls, temaki sushi is a cone-shaped sushi roll made by wrapping rice, fish, and vegetables in a sheet of nori. It's meant to be eaten by hand and is often filled with a variety of ingredients.

Price: Rs.1179

Uramaki Sushi

Uramaki, or inside-out rolls, have rice on the outside and nori on the inside. The rice is often covered with sesame seeds or tobiko (fish roe). Common uramaki rolls include the rainbow roll and spider roll.

Price: Rs.999

Chirashi Sushi

Chirashi sushi consists of a bowl of vinegared rice topped with a variety of sashimi, vegetables, and garnishes. It's a colorful and visually appealing dish.

Price: Rs.1139

Inari Sushi

Inari sushi is made by stuffing seasoned sushi rice into a pocket of marinated and slightly sweet tofu skin called inarizushi-no-moto. It's often enjoyed as a sweet and savory snack.

Price: Rs.1389
